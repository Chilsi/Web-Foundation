Kerala

India's Malabar Coast is home to the state of Kerala. It was established on November 1st, 1956. The main attractions of this state are its calm beaches, serene backwater stretches, lush hill stations, and diverse fauna. Most of Kerala's locations are only a two to four-hour drive apart, which is a significant advantage. Classical art forms, vibrant festivals, and unique cuisine are just a few of the cultural wonders tourists can experience. The traditional Indian medical system of Ayurveda and Panchakarma, an Ayurvedic rejuvenation therapy, have also contributed to Kerala's international recognition as a worthwhile travel destination.

Thanks to Kerala's year-round mild temperature and countless festivals and events, season never ends there.

Athirapilly Falls

Athirappilly Falls, a natural marvel, is located in God's own nation, Kerala. The waterfall, which roars down from an 80-foot height, provides viewers with a calming experience.Visitors to the Athirapally waterfalls might sometimes catch a glimpse of tiny rainbows.

The Athirapally Falls not only enhance the area's beauty but also provide a home for a wide variety of flora and wildlife.

A view that take your breath!

House Boats

As part of Kerala's tradition and heritage, the Kettuvallom (Houseboats in Kerala) was a sizable floating configuration with a considerable load carrying capacity.The city of Alleppey is well-known for its assortment of lakes, lagoons, canals, rivers, and other water features.

A house boat ride frequently becomes a must-do activity in Alleppey because of the surrounding coconut palm trees that line the backwaters.
